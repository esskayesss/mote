# Product

## Vision

Build a meeting orchestration platform that can eventually compete with LiveKit by combining media infrastructure with participant-specific AI assistance.

## Core Experience

- Participants join a shared meeting room backed by an SFU.
- Meetings include live transcription with minimal lag.
- AI observes the meeting stream and private event context in near realtime.
- AI helps individuals with agenda progression, fact checks, corrections, and contextual prompts without broadcasting those interventions to the entire room.

## Near-Term Demo Goal

- A polished demo that supports multiple participants joining a room.
- Media plane handled through mediasoup.
- Separate events channel for non-media control and AI activity.
- A visible agenda teleprompter experience.
- Live transcription as a first-class feature rather than an add-on.
