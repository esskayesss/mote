# Prompts

## AI Assistant Themes

- Agenda manager: identify the current topic, detect drift, and suggest the next discussion checkpoint.
- Fact checker: identify factual claims that need verification and prepare concise participant-specific corrections.
- Meeting coach: identify missed decisions, open loops, and unclear ownership.

## Prompting Constraints

- AI interventions should default to private delivery unless explicitly configured otherwise.
- Prompt design should optimize for low latency and small incremental outputs.
- Outputs must separate observation, confidence, and suggested action.
