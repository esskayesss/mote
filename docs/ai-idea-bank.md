# AI Idea Bank

## Near-Term Ideas

- Personal agenda nudges that suggest the next topic when discussion stalls.
- Private fact-check cards delivered only to the relevant participant.
- Speaker coaching that detects rambling, unaddressed questions, or missed decisions.
- End-of-meeting action extraction with owner and due-date suggestions.

## Mid-Term Ideas

- Retrieval-backed fact checks grounded in meeting uploads or workspace docs.
- Parallel participant copilots with role-specific context windows.
- Conversation heat maps that detect when a topic is under-discussed or overrun.
